Though there is but a single soul upon the stage, standing behind it are the "us" who made this performance possible —- from the family and friends who hold me up, to the strangers who brush past in passing, to every corner and every thing within this world.

Within this work, I encounter my own memories and perceptions. I leave the process of that encounter to the present; I entrust the outcome of that encounter to the future.

No matter what life brings, we can still dance.
