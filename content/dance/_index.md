Hi! I am Leyi, a dancer and choreographer. 

To dance is to speak in a language that precedes words -- that connects the deepest parts of the inner self to the vastness of the world around us. For me, dance is not just a performance; it is a safe space where memory, emotion, and existence meet.

Welcome to my archive of motion. I invite you to watch, to feel, and to find your own story within these frames.
