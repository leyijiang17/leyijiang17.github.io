By the West Lake, the Green Snake and the White Snake are caught by the beauty of the mortal world. One holding an umbrella, the other a fan, the two snakes, close as sisters, are swaying gracefully. But a paper umbrella is never a permanent shelter, and the best is destined to ‘end’.

‘But - you are just a snake.’
