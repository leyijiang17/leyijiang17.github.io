In 'The Museum of Infinite Moments', visitors to a museum are more than just observers, they enact and experience a series of 'encounters'. Each dancer embodies a unique characteristic - from the photo-snapping tourist to an art student lost in a sketch, or a weary writer searching for inspirations. These indentities shape their moves, their interaction with the exhibition, and their reactions to others. Strangers though they may be, these visitors create invisible connections through shared experiences - whether brushing past each other or gazing at the same exhibit.
